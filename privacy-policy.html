Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 15, 2023

At FireLEARN, we are committed to protecting your privacy with the highest standards of data protection. We believe in transparency and want you to understand exactly how we handle your information.

Minimal Data Collection

FireLEARN collects the absolute minimum information necessary to serve our educational mission:

  • Your name (to personalize your experience)
  • Your email address (to send requested books and updates)

We collect this information only when you explicitly request a book. No other personal information is collected at any time.

Strict No-Sharing Policy

FireLEARN operates under a strict data protection policy:

  • We do not share your data with any third parties
  • We do not use any third-party services that access your data
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your information

Your data remains exclusively within FireLEARN's secure systems and is accessible only to essential personnel for service fulfillment.

Data Protection Principles

Data Minimization

We collect only what is essential for book delivery and nothing more.

Strong Security

Your information is protected with industry-standard security measures.

Complete Privacy

Your data is never exposed to advertisers or external services.

Third-Party Requests

FireLEARN maintains a strict policy against external data requests:

Our team will never provide your personal data to any external party. If anyone claims to have requested your information from us, please report it immediately to security@firelearn.org.

What We Collect (And What We Don't)

Name

Collected only for personalization

Email Address

Used only for book delivery

No Other Data

We collect nothing else
